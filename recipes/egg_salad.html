Egg Salad

The best Egg Salad

This easy egg salad recipe makes the best egg salad dish, It starts with perfectly hard boiled eggs and a simple, creamy dressing.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped cucumber
  • 3 tablespoons ranch dressing
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Start by boiling your eggs.
  2. Coarsely chop the egg whites and yolks.
  3. Use a good mayonnaise in your egg salad.
  4. Combine eggs, cucumber, ranch dressing, mustard, salt, an black pepper in a bowl.
  5. Season with a pich of table salt and white pepper.
