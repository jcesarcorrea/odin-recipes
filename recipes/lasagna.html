Lasagna

The best lasagna

Lasagna is a type of pasta. It usually served in overlapping sheets interspersed with layers of ingredients to taste, most frequently meat in bolognese and bechamel sauce.

Its origin is Italian an the dish usually prepared with meat is called baked lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Make the Meat Sauce.
  2. Cook Your Noodles.
  3. Prep the Cheese and Finish the Sauce.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
