Waldorf Salad

The best waldorf salad

A Waldorf salad is a fruit and nut salad generally made of celety, fresh apples, walnuts, and grapes, dressed in mayonnaise, and traditionally served on a bed of lettuce as an appetizer or an light meal. The apples, celery, and grapes can all be green, which harmonizes the color palette of the dish.

The Waldorf Salad is named for the Waldorf-Astoria hotel in New York City, where it was created for a charity ball given in honor of the St. Mary's Hospital for Children on March 13, 1896.

Ingredients

  • Tart apples
  • Celery
  • Walnuts or pecans
  • Grapes
  • Lemon Juice
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Mayonnaise
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon, and salt together in a serving bowl.
  2. Stir in apples, celery, walnuts and raisins.
  3. Chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
